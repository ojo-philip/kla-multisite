About Us


KLA, a pro leadership non profit organization that is founded to equip high school leavers and fresh students of tertiary institutions with true leadership skills through her various life changing conferences, summits, fellowships and SDG oriented social impact projects.

vision

To be the foremost international, pro leadership and non-profit organisation that transforms African youths into true leaders.

mission

  • To admit fresh tertiary students as fellows of the Freshire Campus Fellowship every yea
  • To admit senior secondary/high school students as fellows of the Highsphire Network of Promising Leaders every year
  • To provide lifetime mentorship in all aspects of life for every HNPL and FCF fellows.
  • To make leaders relevant throughout their lifetime by providing mentorship to younger leaders
  • To positively impact the world via problem solving innovations and societal-improvement fellowship projects executed by fellows
  • Ultimately, to develop and coach leaders that will respond effectively to political, business, spiritual, financial and educational problems of their families, communities and nations.

values

  • Exceptional knowledge & wisdom
  • Excellent creativity & Innovation

  • Great ability to Influence
  • Premium value on relationships
  • Effective Communication
  • Great sense of timeliness
