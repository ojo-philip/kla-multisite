ORGANOGRAM


BOARD OF TRUSTEES

  • This is the highest decision-making body of the organisation
  • Takes administrative decisions for the organisation
  • Gives ultimate sanction for every misconduct or negligence within the organisation
  • Members and executives can submit an appeal to the BoT if they have any concerns about anything and anyone within the organisation

EXECUTIVES

  • The executive position is for a year (January to December) after which the executive can decide whether to continue or to hand over.
  • Every executive must take full responsibility for his/her members
  • Negligence of duty without appropriate permission will attract penalties as determined by the Board of Trustees

ROLES OF EXECUTIVES

President/Team Lead (TL)
  • The overall head of the organization
  • Ensures that the other executives work in line with the organization's goal and vision

Vice President
  • To assist the President in the supervisory role
  • Can act on behalf of the President once he/she has the directive to do so

General Secretary (GS)
  • Keeps important documents of the organization electronically
  • Write the briefs of every meeting and store them electronically
  • Submit monthly summary to the president on achievements and limitations

Content Directorate(CD)
  • Create the content for every graphic, clip, bulletin, seminar, summit, conference, social impact project etc of the organization
  • Work closely with other executives to generate content
  • All contents must go through the Editorial directorate for proper review and correction.
  • Submit monthly summary to the president on directorate achievements and limitations

Design and videography Directorate (DVD)
  • Should have graphic designers and videography
  • Transform the content into graphic designs and/or video clips
  • Submit monthly summary to the president on directorate achievements and limitations

Editorial Directorate(ED)
  • Review every document or content created by the General secretary, content directorate and DVD for correctness before they are stored or published.
  • Submit monthly summary to the president on directorate achievements and limitations

Publicity Directorate (PD)
  • Publication and dissemination of graphics, bulletins, clips, tweets and posts etc
  • Manages the social media handles of the organization ( Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, youtube)
  • Manages the website of the organization for updating events, activities and other contents
  • Submit monthly summary to the president on directorate achievements and limitations

Grants Directorate(GD)
  • Search for available grants to fund the various projects and programs of the organization
  • Work with the general secretary and editorial directors in applying for the grants
  • Follow up on grants applied for and periodically look for other grants
  • Win grants for the organization
  • Submit monthly summary to the president on directorate achievements and limitations

Finance Directorate (FD)
  • Keep a record of the organization income and expenditures
  • Source for funds (aside from grants) to finance various projects and programs of the organization.
  • Be in charge of monthly contributions from members
  • Submit monthly summary to the president on directorate achievements and limitations

Special Duty Directorate (SDD)
  • Manages the distribution of letters
  • Sets up the venue of events
  • Work with the president and general secretary to meet with partners.
  • Performs other activities that are not in the jurisdiction of any directorate
  • Submit monthly summary to the president on directorate achievements and limitations

Human Resources Directorate (HRD)
  • Ensure the welfare of every executive and member of the organization
  • Recruit and train new members for the organization
  • Ensure regular and periodic training of executives and members of the organization on various things like our values, leadership, soft skills, hard skills and other life-transforming seminars
  • Ensure refreshments for every event or meeting.
  • Submit monthly summary to the president on directorate achievements and limitations
